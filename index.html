Chavela

A Beginners Fitness App

I have recently become interested in fitness and when I first started I had no idea where to begin. I happen to to be in my coding school at the time which led me to build this app for my final project.

Chavela works by having the user fill out a couple of questions that ask their goals. Based on the results the app will build a custom program that allows you to keep track of your workouts and remove the ones you have completed. The app also has a resource page that links to several places to go and research more on nutrition and programs
